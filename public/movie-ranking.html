The Best Movies According to Injamam

My top 3 movies of all-time.

3 idiots

I like the movie 3 Idiots because it blends humor, emotion, and powerful life lessons about following your passion.

Taare Zameen Par

I like because it beautifully portrays the struggles of a misunderstood child and the power of compassion in education.

Lagaan

I like Lagaan because it inspires with its story of unity, courage, and the triumph of the underdog against all odds.
